# Budget Simulator

Interactive budget visualization and policy simulation tool for citizens. See the national budget, customize your own allocations, and explore financial consequences of policy proposals in real-time.

## Mission

Demystify government budgets for everyday people during election year. Enable citizens to:
- **Visualize** current budget structure and spending
- **Simulate** custom budget allocations
- **Explore** policy proposals and their financial impacts
- **Compare** different scenarios side-by-side

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** SvelteKit + Svelte + TypeScript
- **Styling:** TailwindCSS + DaisyUI
- **Backend:** Supabase (or mock for MVP)
- **Data:** Budget datasets, policy proposals
- **Hosting:** Vercel
